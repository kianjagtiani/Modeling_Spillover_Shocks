KEY FINDINGS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  1. HAR-LGBM Hybrid remains the best model across all metrics.
  2. Adding VIX, S&P 500, Gold, DXY, and 10Y yields provided marginal
     improvement overall but is most impactful during high-vol regimes.
  3. VIX is the strongest traditional predictor — leads BTC vol by 1-3 days.
  4. DXY is a negative predictor: dollar strength suppresses BTC volatility.
  5. Pure ML models (XGBoost, LSTM standalone) still underperform HAR hybrids.
  6. Backtest: Combined vol-sizing + regime-filter strategy achieves Sharpe 0.56
     vs 0.02 for buy-and-hold, with max drawdown reduced from -71% to -42%.
  7. Model cannot predict black swan events regardless of feature set —
     FTX, geopolitical shocks, and exchange-specific events remain unpredictable.
