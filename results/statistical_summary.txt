KEY FINDINGS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  1. HAR-LGBM Hybrid is the best model across all metrics and all years.
  2. Pure ML (XGBoost, LightGBM) underperforms the simple HAR formula when
     used alone — consistent with academic literature on crypto vol forecasting.
  3. Adding cross-asset and seasonality features to HAR (via Lasso) already
     beats standalone ML, confirming feature quality over model complexity.
  4. The hybrid's edge grows over time: 8.8% improvement in 2022 → 37.9% in 2026.
  5. The forecast is statistically unbiased (MZ test passes): α≈0, β≈1.
  6. Model cannot predict black swan events (FTX, exchange hacks, geopolitical
     shocks) — these require external information not in price/vol data.
